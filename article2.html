Top Courses After 12th: Finding Your Fit at Haridwar University

Published on March 28, 2026

The transition from school to college is a defining step. To help students stay ahead in a rapidly changing job market, Haridwar University (HU) has integrated Artificial Intelligence (AI) into almost every discipline.

The Tech Path: B.Tech (Hons.)

HU’s B.Tech programs now feature enhanced specializations and industry certifications. Choose from Computer Science (AI & ML, Cyber Security, Data Science), Electronics (with VLSI Certification), or Core Engineering (all with AI integration).

The Corporate Path: BBA & B.Com

For those aiming for leadership roles, the Business Studies wing offers a tech-forward approach with BBA and B.Com with AI.

The Healthcare Path: Pharmacy & Nursing

The Allied Health Sciences department is fully accredited by PCI and INC, offering B.Sc Nursing and B.Pharm with AI integration.

The Agricultural Path: B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture

A premier environment for agricultural study with ICAR Certification, focusing on smart farming and sustainable agri-tech.

The Digital Path: BCA & Computer Science

Focused software and digital service sector degrees in AI & ML, Data Science, and Cyber Security.
