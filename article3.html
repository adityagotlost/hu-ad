Beyond the Classroom: The "L&T and IBM Advantage" at Haridwar University

Published on March 28, 2026

What sets Haridwar University apart from traditional institutions? It’s their Experiential Learning Model. This blog explores how HU bridges the gap between being a student and becoming an industry leader.

Strategic Industry Tie-ups

Haridwar University has pioneered the concept of "Industry-Powered Labs." For instance, engineering students work on the same equipment and software used by L&T, giving them a massive edge during interviews.

Mandatory Internships

HU encourages a 6-9 month internship period. Students often receive stipends ranging from ₹18,000 to ₹50,000, allowing them to earn while they learn and secure PPOs.

Soft Skills & Personality Development

The Corporate Resource Centre (CRC) at HU doesn't just bring companies to campus; they prepare you for them through workshops, mock interviews, and personality development.

Conclusion: Haridwar University provides an education that is practical, modern, and result-oriented.
